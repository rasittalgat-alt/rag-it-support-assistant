# IT Support Service Level Agreement (SLA) – Excerpt

This document describes the main SLA targets for incident handling.

## Priority Levels

- **P1 – Critical**
  - Complete service outage or major business impact.
  - Initial response time: **15 minutes**.
  - Target resolution time: **4 hours**.

- **P2 – High**
  - Significant degradation of service with workaround available.
  - Initial response time: **1 hour**.
  - Target resolution time: **8 business hours**.

- **P3 – Medium**
  - Standard incidents affecting a single user or small group.
  - Initial response time: **4 business hours**.
  - Target resolution time: **3 business days**.

- **P4 – Low**
  - Minor issues, cosmetic defects, general questions.
  - Initial response time: **1 business day**.
  - Target resolution time: **5 business days**.

## Working Hours

- IT Support service desk working hours:
  - Monday–Friday, 9:00–18:00 (excluding public holidays).
- Incidents reported outside working hours are processed on the next business day, unless classified as P1.
