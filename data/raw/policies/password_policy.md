# Corporate Password Policy (Excerpt)

This document describes basic password requirements for corporate accounts.

## Password Requirements

- Minimum length: **12 characters**.
- Must contain characters from at least **three** of the following groups:
  - Uppercase letters (A–Z)
  - Lowercase letters (a–z)
  - Digits (0–9)
  - Special characters (!, @, #, $, %, etc.)
- Must not contain:
  - Your username
  - Your first name or last name
  - Obvious words such as "password" or "1234"

## Password Expiration

- Passwords must be changed at least once every **90 days**.
- Users will receive notification emails before password expiration.

## Account Lockout

- After **5** failed login attempts, the account is locked for **15 minutes**.
- IT Support can unlock an account earlier upon user request after identity verification.
